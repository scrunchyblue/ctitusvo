Questions people usually ask me.

Do you authorize the use of your voice for AI training?
No, I do not authorize the use of my voice for AI training, voice synthesis, or any machine learning purposes without explicit written consent and appropriate compensation.
Do you offer voice coaching?
Yes! I offer one-on-one voice coaching sessions for aspiring voice actors. We can work on technique, delivery, home studio setup, and building your demo reel.
Can you help me with my home studio?
Absolutely. I can provide guidance on equipment selection, acoustic treatment, and optimizing your recording space for professional-quality audio.
Can you help me produce a commercial demo?
Yes, I offer demo production services. We'll select copy, direct your performance, and produce a polished demo that showcases your unique voice and range.
Do you have editing rates?
Yes, I offer audio editing services at competitive rates. Contact me for a quote based on your project's scope and requirements.
Can you direct?
Yes! With over 20 years of acting experience, I can provide professional direction for your voiceover projects to help you get the best possible performance.
